}



2 Get Customer

GET:http://localhost:8080/api/customers



3 Create Policy

POST:http://localhost:8080/api/policies

{
  "policyNumber": "POL1001",
  "policyType": "Health",
  "premiumAmount": 2000,
  "coverageAmount": 500000,
  "startDate": "2026-01-01",
  "endDate": "2027-01-01",
  "customerId": 1
}



4 Get Policy

GET:http://localhost:8080/api/policies/1



5 Update Policy

PUT:http://localhost:8080/api/policies/1
{
  "policyNumber": "POL1001",
  "policyType": "Life",
  "premiumAmount": 3000,
  "coverageAmount": 800000,
  "startDate": "2026-01-01",
  "endDate": "2028-01-01"
}


6 Filter Policies

GET: http://localhost:8080/api/policies/type/Health


7 Pagination

GET:http://localhost:8080/api/policies/premium?min=1000&max=5000

8 Cancel Policy

GET:http://localhost:8080/api/policies?page=0&size=5&sortBy=premiumAmount&direction=asc

DELETE:http://localhost:8080/api/policies/1
